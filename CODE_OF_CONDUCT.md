# Code of Conduct

This project uses a direct, technical collaboration style. Contributors are
expected to keep discussion respectful, factual, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Disagree with technical claims by explaining evidence and tradeoffs.
- Respect different levels of experience and different professional contexts.
- Keep reviews specific to code, documentation, tests, and project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or sustained disruption.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Deliberately derailing project discussions or review processes.

## Reporting

Until a dedicated private conduct-reporting channel is published, open a
[minimal repository issue](https://github.com/frames-sg/j2k/issues/new) asking
for private maintainer contact. Do not include incident details or personal
information in that issue. Maintainers will review
reports, limit disclosure to what is necessary, and may remove comments, close
issues, or restrict participation when needed to protect the project
community. Vulnerability reporting and response expectations remain defined
separately in `SECURITY.md`.
